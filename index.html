Before You Begin

This experience uses bright, rapidly flashing light. Photic stimulation can trigger seizures in people who are susceptible, including those who have never had one.

Do not use this if you:

  • Have epilepsy or a seizure disorder
  • Are pregnant
  • Are intoxicated, including from medications that lower the seizure threshold
  • Have a heart or vestibular condition

Sit or lie down somewhere safe. Stop immediately if you feel unwell, dizzy, or off. The session can be ended any time by tapping the screen or pressing space.

This is not a medical device, not a therapy, and not a substitute for one.
